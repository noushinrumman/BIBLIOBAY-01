About us

BiblioBay is a premier bookstore and online marketplace based in Chattogram, Bangladesh, committed to connecting readers with a rich and diverse collection of books — from timeless classics to the latest bestsellers, in both print and digital formats.


Our mission is to inspire a love of reading within our community by making quality literature easily accessible and by championing the work of local authors. At BiblioBay, we believe that every book opens the door to a new world of imagination, knowledge, and possibility.
